Studies

I studied in two different schools/highschools, Jesuitinas and Compañía de María, where I enjoyed doing science, math, english and technical drawing. During my last years of school I realized that I wanted to do something with computers, so I started spending my free time learning more about technology.

After that, I graduated in Computer Engineering from Universidade da Coruña. The education was mainly Java-based programming, but I also learned about C, C#, SQL, UML and more. My master thesis was titled "Enforcing Resource Usage Limits in a HPC-as--Service Cloud Platform" and it got a score of 9.5/10.
